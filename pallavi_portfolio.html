02 / 07R · Statistics
Cardiovascular Disease Risk Factor Analysis

Investigated the association between cardiovascular disease and five risk factors — gender, age, blood pressure, cholesterol, and diabetes — using Chi-square tests, independent t-tests, and Wilcoxon rank-sum tests. Males showed significantly higher heart disease prevalence (Cramer's V = 0.29). A logistic regression model combining age, sex, and blood pressure achieved 68.89% accuracy and outperformed a sex-only baseline model (likelihood ratio p < 0.05).

07 / 07Capstone · NLP + ML
Pregnancy Drug Safety Classification (Capstone)

Developed a supervised ML and NLP framework to predict FDA Pregnancy Drug Safety Categories (A, B, C, D, X) using structured drug metadata and unstructured side-effect narratives from Drugs.com (n=2,931). Applied scispaCy for NER and NMF for topic modeling, extracting 20 coherent clinical themes. Among eight models tested, the SMOTE-enhanced ANN achieved the best performance: 90.94% accuracy, macro F1 = 0.92, and AUC-ROC = 0.98. SHAP analysis highlighted thyroid/respiratory drugs, hyperthyroidism, hay fever, and keywords like "insomnia" and "hair" as top predictors, validating clinical interpretability.
